The Blind Carbon Copy module injects a BCC header in all e-mails sent by
Drupal, which allows you to receive copies of all e-mails sent by your website.


== INSTALLATION ==

Unpack the module into sites/all/modules and enable on admin/build/modules.


== CONFIGURATION ==

Visit admin/settings/bcc on your site and configure the module. If you do
not have access to this page, check that your account has the "administer
site configuration" permission.
